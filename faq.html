Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering

1. How do I place an order?
To place an order, please visit our product catalog, select the items you want, and add them to your shopping cart. Then, proceed to the checkout page to complete your purchase.
2. What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for products?
The MOQ for our products varies. You can find the MOQ for each item listed in the product details.
3. Can I modify or cancel an order after it's been placed?
Order modifications and cancellations are possible, but you should contact our customer support as soon as possible to make the necessary changes.

Shipping and Delivery

4. What are the shipping options available?
We offer various shipping options, including standard and expedited shipping. Shipping costs and delivery times will vary depending on your location and the chosen shipping method.
5. How can I track my order?
Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number and a link to track your package online. You can also check your order status by logging into your account.

Returns and Refunds

6. What is your return policy?
We have a 30-day return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange. Please review our Return Policy for more details.
7. How long does it take to process a refund?
Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. The exact time may vary based on your payment method and bank processing times.

Account Management

8. How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by visiting the login page and clicking the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions to reset your password.
9. How can I update my account information?
You can update your account information by logging into your account and navigating to the "Account Settings" or "Profile" section. Make the desired changes and save your updates.
